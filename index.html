Heart stealing thief

This person is selfish, fragile and sensitive. Always angry or sad about small things. He is not worthy of being loved, but he has never lacked the love from friends or lovers, so he thinks he is a lucky person.

He likes reading books and watching movies. But he never tried to write a book or make a movie in the past, because he felt that he was not a genius and could not create something that others liked. He likes shopping. Every time he pays, he can clearly feel that there is something in this world that really belongs to him. He is lazy and never remembers the names of strangers. Even if he meets those people every day, he will only say hello to them. He likes to sit on a chair in the park at night and listen to the music in his headphones. He doesn't look at the stars or the moon. He always looks at the ground, the concrete ground without grass or leaves. His favorite band is My Bloody Valentine and Nirvana. He once tried to form a band and ended up in failure because he was coming to the United States for his university.

He felt that he was a complete loser, fleeing from one city to another. He met many interesting people, made friends with them and then left in silence, breaking their hearts. Who will be the next?
